About me

Who am I?

My name is Matthew Rodrigues. I'm a second year undergraduate student at the University of the West Indies pursuing a double major in Mathematics and Economics. I have a passion for math and helping others find that same passion.

Teaching Philosophy

While I have done well on my exams (1500 on the SAT and 17 grade ones across CSEC and CAPE) I find other qualities to be much more valuable in being a good tutor. I find being able to adapt to a specific student's learning style, being patient, and having a passion for the material are far more useful and I've tried my best to apply and improve on them throughout my tutoring experiences.
